Hello and welcome to my page! My name is Mackenzie Rayfield.

Art has been a passion of mine since I was young. When I was 10, my best friend and I started painting together. Soon after that, I discovered a love and a niche for landscape painting.

Painting is where I find peace and connect with God and nature. You'll notice most of my work is inspired by the outdoors, because I love capturing the beauty of the world around us. My hope is that when you view my artwork, you're able to pause, slow down, and notice the beauty of God's creation.

Psalm 19:1 - "The heavens proclaim the glory of God. The skies display his craftsmanship."

If there's a "moment" in your world you'd like me to capture, I'd love to hear from you.
